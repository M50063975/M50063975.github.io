Virtual Reality Exposure Therapy

Source 1

URL: https://www.webmd.com/anxiety-panic/virtual-reality-exposure-therapy-overview
Site Author: WebMD Editorial Team
Abstract:
This WebMD article provides an overview of Virtual Reality Exposure Therapy (VRET), an emerging treatment for anxiety disorders. It explains how VRET uses virtual environments to safely expose patients to feared situations, aiding in the reduction of anxiety symptoms. The piece discusses the effectiveness of VRET in treating conditions like phobias and PTSD, highlighting its potential benefits and the need for further research to expand its applications.

URL: https://www.brainline.org/treatment-hub-treating-brain-injury-and-ptsd/virtual-reality-exposure-therapy-vret
Site Author: BrainLine Editorial Team
Abstract:
This BrainLine article explores how Virtual Reality Exposure Therapy (VRET) is used to treat PTSD and traumatic brain injury. It describes how VRET creates controlled virtual environments that mimic real-life trauma triggers, allowing patients to confront and process their experiences safely. The article emphasizes the therapy's effectiveness in reducing stress responses and improving coping mechanisms for individuals with PTSD.
